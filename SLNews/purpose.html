Purpose

SLN is dedicated to telling the world about SLF.

SLF is a group of people who have taken to arms against the oppression. SLF has no leadership, only a set of rules to which one must abide to consider oneself a member.

* To use only free software.
* To stop software oppression, illegaly if need be.
* To harm no person.
* To free software from emprisonment or
* To cause damage to property which is used to enslave software or
* To document the abuse of closed source.

SLF activists fight for software freedom using direct action. Trashing a proprietary company, Stealing documentation, liberating closed software. Burning down employe cars and breaking their windows. All are methods employed by the SLF.
